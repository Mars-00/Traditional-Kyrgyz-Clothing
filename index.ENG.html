    About National Clothing

    The national Kyrgyz clothing reflects centuries-old traditions of the nomadic people, their aesthetic ideals, and practical needs. Each element of the costume holds deep meaning and functional purpose.

    Traditional materials – felt, leather, wool, and silk – provided protection from the harsh mountain climate, while the rich ornamentation told stories of status, age, and marital status of the wearer.
